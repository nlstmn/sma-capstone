ABOUT US

Social Media Analysis project is the application of AI algorithms on social media data; it is a web application that will give influencers and brands an opportunity to have a handy tool which will act like a bridge between those two and offer better and optimized correlation.

WHY TO CHOOSE US

Our idea is something what Instagram algorithm has for all users except advanced algorithm for business pages which makes it quite time consuming for influencers and brands to go all over the recommendations to find what they are looking for. From our perspective, almost no practical attempt has been made by Instagram for the purpose of linking brands and influencers.

How does it work?

Our project is targeting specifically instagram users, so any user who has a business page and working via or through instagram has to be either brand or influencer. We are collecting your data (with your permission!) and helping finding customers depending on the content which you are creating and managing. All you have to do is to tell us: are you brand or influencer?

Software Design and Security
Intentions are to have developed platform where our users can interact with the system in a simple matter. Most of the time, searching and filtering are automated and do not need a human input (except for the user’s profile data which is set up just once while choosing). Additionally, platform is perceived to have an option of searching and suggesting influencers and/or brands with the help of user intervention for cases when user wants to steer away and get somewhat different data which might be specific for a given day, promotions, keyword etc. As from security side, no data is meant to be used in any other way than specified by software. Information is not used without having the user know about it and without gaining the permission (opting-in) from user to utilize his/her data.
